## üí° Usage Recommendations

### **Before the Meeting:**

1. **Customize for audience:**
   - Update Slide 13 (ecosystem) with their specific stack
   - Adjust Slide 9 (use cases) to match their industry
   - Prioritize Slide 14 (roadmap) based on their needs

2. **Prepare for questions:**
   - Review Appendix D (competitive scripts)
   - Have pricing tiers ready (not in deck per your request)
   - Know their current tools and pain points

3. **Load relevant sections:**
   - Technical audience? Include Appendix A-B
   - Competitive deal? Include Appendix D
   - Quick demo? Stick to slides 1-16

---

### **During the Meeting:**

1. **Start strong:**
   - Slide 1: Show ecosystem compatibility
   - Slide 2: Confirm their pain points
   - Slide 4: Lead with AI (3 seconds hook)

2. **Adapt in real-time:**
   - Skip slides if already covered in conversation
   - Jump to use cases (Slide 9) when relevant
   - Show deployment diagram (Slide 10) when data sovereignty comes up

3. **Handle objections:**
   - Competitive question? Jump to Slide 8 or Appendix D
   - Security concern? Deep-dive on Slide 11
   - Cost concern? Emphasize Slide 15 (predictable pricing)

### **After the Meeting:**

1. **Send deck within 24 hours**
2. **Include only relevant appendices**
3. **Add custom slides if needed:**
   - ROI calculation for their scenario
   - Specific use case from their organization
   - Timeline for their implementation
