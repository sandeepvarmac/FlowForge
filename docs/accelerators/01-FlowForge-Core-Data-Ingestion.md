## How We Compare

| Tool | What They Do | What FlowForge Does Better |
|------|-------------|---------------------------|
| **ADF / AWS Glue** | Powerful orchestration, requires coding | Simple UI, AI-assisted, quality built-in |
| **Fivetran / Airbyte** | Replicate data from SaaS, files, and databases into your warehouse | Adds **medallion modeling** (Bronze/Silver/Gold), inline quality/quarantine, and governance on top of raw ingestion |
| **dbt** | SQL-based transformation on existing warehouse | Handles the full journey: load → clean → organize (sits *before* dbt in your stack) |
| **Informatica / Talend** | Broad enterprise integration suites | Simpler, faster to deploy, focused on medallion ingestion |

**Best For:** Teams who want clean, organized data pipelines without the complexity of enterprise tools.
